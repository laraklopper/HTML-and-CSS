MY HOBBY

My name is Lara Klopper, I was born and live in South Africa. My hobbies include is photography and hiking. Since I was young, I have always been enthusiastic about wildlife and landscape photography and have always had a strong interest in nature conservation and geography.

I have been lucky enough to have visited many game reserves in South Africa and taken many photos as an amateur photographer.
